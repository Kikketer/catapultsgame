Timer App

Catapults! is a crazy fun game that takes less than 20 minutes to play and even less time to learn.

You have 30 seconds to place your pieces and create at least one complete castle. Once complete you place as many catapults as you can fit inside.

Once catapults are placed, roll dice and see how many walls of your opponents you can destroy.

Build and rebuild the best castle to win!
